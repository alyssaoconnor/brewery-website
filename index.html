Any way you look at it—down the end of your nose in a mug or glass held up to the light—it doesn’t matter, you can’t beat fresh, local, small batch microbrew. It’s our passion and we’re inviting you to give it a go.

Give the descriptions a read, download a recipe or two, check out what we think these beers are best paired with, and most of all stop in to really taste what we’re all about. Bottom line, we brew beer, not reading material. The lights are on, the glasses cold and the taps open, what other reason do you need?

About Us

We are a small batch craft brewery in Denver, Colorado, with big dreams, a big property, and carefully crafted beers.

Our passion for people, happiness and the magic behind making beer inspires our team to bring the community together, to celebrate life and cherish the ritual of craft beer.

We have ping pong tables, corn hole, pickle ball and coming soon… mini-golf.

Beer Hall

We allow well behaved dogs on short leashes in the taproom. They are allowed both inside the taproom and outside on our patio, however we prefer all 4 paws on the floor and not in seats or on tables. We also ask that you do not tie leashes to tables or chairs, and that one hand be on the leash at all times.

We ask you to remember that children need to be attended at all times. Failure to keep a watchful eye on your child will unfortunately mean we have to ask you to leave for the safety of both your children and our staff.

Lastly, we make our designated space available for private events, ensuring a fun, thoughtful, and hospitable atmosphere for your event. Please fill out the form below with your info & request.
